About

Greetings and welcome to my homepage! My name is Jurjan de Graaf. I develop analytics and software solutions for grid operators and energy suppliers. I am Amsterdam based and hold a MSc in mechanical engineering from TU Delft. I am committed to contribute to the energy transition and fostering a sustainable world.

Next to my professional endeavours, I actively enjoy the outdoors and like to go kitesurfing whenever it is windy. Other days I can be found rowing on the river Amstel. During quieter times, I appreciate wandering through a museum or delving into literature, with my current focus on the philosophy of simple living.

Have a look around and feel free to contact me via email.
