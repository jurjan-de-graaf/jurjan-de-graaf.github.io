About

Greetings! Welcome to my website. My name is Jurjan de Graaf, and I am a data engineer specialized in developing data infrastructure for organisations. In addition I offer consulting for building data ecosystems from the early stage into a mature data landscape. I am Amsterdam based and hold a MSc in Mechanical Engineering from TU Delft. I am committed to contributing to the energy transition and building a sustainable world.

Next to my professional endeavours, I actively enjoy the outdoors and like to go kitesurfing whenever it is windy. Other days I can be found rowing on the river Amstel. During quieter times, I appreciate wandering through a museum or delving into literature, with my current focus on the philosophy of simple living.

Have a look around and feel free to contact me via email.
